FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

Everything you need to know before we start building together.

How long does a website take to build?+

It depends on the size. A basic 5-page website takes 5–10 working days. A business website with booking systems takes 2–4 weeks. A full e-commerce store or mobile app can take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start — no surprises.

Do you offer payment plans?+

Yes. We work on a 50% deposit upfront and 50% on delivery model. For larger projects (R5,000+) we can discuss a 3-part payment plan: 40% to start, 30% at midpoint, 30% on delivery. WhatsApp us to chat about what works for you.

Do I need to buy hosting and a domain separately?+

We handle everything for you. Domain registration costs around R150–R250/year and hosting from R100–R400/month depending on your needs. We'll recommend the best options for your budget and set it all up — you don't need to be technical.

Can you maintain my site after it's done?+

Absolutely — that's what our R300/month maintenance plan covers. We handle updates, security checks, backups, and small content changes. You focus on running your business, we keep your site running smoothly. Support is via WhatsApp.

Will my website work on phones?+

100% — every site we build is fully mobile responsive. Most of your customers will visit on their phones, so we design mobile-first. Your site will look sharp on any screen, from a small Android to a large desktop monitor.

What do you need from me to get started?+

Just contact us and we'll chat! We'll ask about your business, what you need, and your budget. From there we send you a simple quote. Once you're happy and the deposit is paid, we start building. You provide your logo (if you have one), photos, and business info — we do the rest.

Do you work with clients outside Langaville?+

Yes! We're based in Langaville but we serve clients across Tsakane, Kwa-Thema, Springs, Brakpan, and all of Ekurhuleni. We also work with clients across South Africa — everything is done remotely via WhatsApp, email, and video call.

Can you design a logo or brand identity for my business?+

Yes — our creative team can design a logo, brand colours, and visual identity that makes your business look professional and memorable. Branding packages start from R350. Ask us when you get in touch and we'll bundle it with your project.
